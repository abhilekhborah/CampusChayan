Some other notable polytechnic colleges in Rajasthan include Northern Institute of Engineering Technical Campus (Alwar), Suresh Gyan Vihar University (Jaipur), and Bhawani Institute of Technical Education (Nagaur). The government also operates several polytechnic colleges across Rajasthan, such as Government Polytechnic College (Alwar), Government Women Polytechnic College (Ajmer), and Government Polytechnic College (Jodhpur).
